Enter the appropriate import parms. To get details on the import parms hit the 'Help' button.



To get a list of tables to import hit the 'Get Tables' button.

To get a list of columns to set index field with hit the 'Get Columns' button.

To get a list of date time formats hit the 'Date Time Formats' button.. The formats are 'strftime' formats that can be cut and pasted in the appropriate parm.
